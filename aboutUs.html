About Us

A little info about us

Environmental Friendly Campaign is an Irish non-profit and charitable organization based in Cork, Ireland. It was established back in 2000. It is the largest organization in Ireland with over 7 offices across Ireland and the United Kingdom.

Frequently Asked Questions

Question: Do you guys plan to start selling some merchandise?

Answer: Yes! We are actually planning to launch some t-shirts and caps next month.

Question: Are you guys a non-profit organization?

Answer: Yup! We are and always will be a non-profit organization. We believe this organization has much bigger purposes other than generating profit.

Question: How to know if I have won the competition?

Answer: So if you have checked the box where you agree to receive updates from us, you will know the results by then. If not, you can always refer to our twitter page.

Question: Who can participate in the cleaning up event?

Answer: Anyone! No matter if you are a kid or a granny, it will be grand if you can help out!

Question: How do I participate in the cleaning up event?

Answer: Well, you just do. Show up on the spot, we will provide you a free t-shirt, and all the tools you need.

Question: I can't show up on any of the cleaning up events, but I still wanna help out, what can I do?

Answer: We are glad that you do, you can actualy donate to our charity with any amount.
